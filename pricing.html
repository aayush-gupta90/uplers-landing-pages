FAQ

Pricing questions, answered.

The details founders ask before choosing a model.

Traditional agencies charge a single high success fee regardless of role or stage. Uplers prices each model around the value it delivers — a flat per-role fee for AI-led Curated Sourcing, a success fee for high-touch Full Service Hiring, and a monthly rate for Contract Talent — so you only pay for the level of involvement you actually need.
Early-stage and lean teams hiring less complex roles usually start with Curated Sourcing. Startups and product companies hiring senior, niche, or business-critical roles lean on Full Service Hiring. Product companies and GCCs scaling execution capacity choose Contract Talent. Not sure? The comparison table above — or a quick chat — will point you to the right fit.
₹25,000–₹50,000 per role covers AI-led sourcing and matching, a curated profile shortlist, skill and mindset alignment, and high-intent profiles only. Your team stays in control of interviews and closure. The exact fee varies by role complexity and experience level.
It's 10%–15% of the hire's annual CTC, charged only when the right candidate joins. The rate varies by seniority and complexity. You get a dedicated principal recruiter who owns sourcing, evaluation, pitching, coordination, offer management, and joining support end to end.
Contract Talent starts from $2,500 per month per resource, varying by role, skillset, experience, and engagement duration. It includes product-ready talent, flexible engagement models, faster onboarding, and full payroll and compliance management.
Yes. Full Service Hiring includes replacement cover as part of the engagement, and Contract Talent includes replacement support per contract. Curated Sourcing is a sourcing-only model, so closure and replacement stay on your side.
